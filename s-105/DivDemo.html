tollywood heros introduction

In Telugu cinema (Tollywood), a hero's introduction scene is a moment of cinematic elevation. Directors use it to establish the character's power, charisma, and emotional depth—often within the first few minutes of the film. These scenes are crafted with dramatic visuals, powerful music, and stylized action to leave a lasting impression.
